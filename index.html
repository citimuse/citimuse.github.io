NFT Utility

NFTs represent a phase in the Citimuse project. Citimuse is creating a marketplace that will birth vibrant economies, support creators, and allow people to trade seamlessly. During our private sale, 100 whitelist spots shall be available. The marketplace’s NFT utility is spread across multiple areas to offer participants a great experience while shopping. NFT utility options include free shipping of Citimuse items for one year after launch, access to DAO on the Discord server, access to updates channel, monthly giveaways, and a 50% discount on fees for items on the website.

DAO

Citimuse is a decentralized marketplace that intends to offer its community myriads of benefits. The marketplace will share revenue with holders in its commitment to true decentralization. The DAO will also be the epicenter of the project, with the responsibility to ensure quality listings in the marketplace. Functions of DAO include dispute settlements, voting on store creations, and ban appeals. Buying NFTs could be a way to join the DAO. You’ll also earn a commission on the listings — as a DAO member.

Token Utility

Citimuse’s tokenomics will offer participants multiple rewards for their holdings. Top ten token holders at the end of the month join the DAO. You get a discount on fees when you make purchases on the items using your tokens. This bolsters your purchase capacity and allows you to buy more for less in the marketplace.

Project Roadmap

Q1 2022
NFT Creation — A total of 1500 NFTs unlocking multiple benefits shall be created on Solana. Holding the NFT shall grant access to various benefits including free shipping, DAO, and airdrops.
Q2 2022
The NFT launch shall be leveraged to fundraise for the Citimuse marketplace. The cumulation of the 1500 NFTs’ mint shall form one of the financial streams to bring the marketplace ideas to fruition. Additionally, the development team shall also be expanded to take on more growth functions and execute the project accordingly. There shall be a private sale with 100 whitelist spots available for grabs. This month shall also witness the release of the marketplace’s initial version to DAO. During this stage, creators can pre-list their items on the marketplace before it fully goes live. The token presale shall also take place.
Q3 2022
DAO has a responsibility to vote on listings and dispute settlements. The voting systems and procedures shall be established. Each proper vote shall also qualify DAO members to earn a commission. DAO ranking operates to indicate the difference in each member’s influence. The relevant systems shall be established, concerning ranking to influence earning percentages, total vote pool, losing ranks, and rank reset.
Q4 2022
Alpha release to DAO members. At this stage, the marketplace has passed quality assurance testing and will be released to DAO members to have a feel.
Q1 2023
The year shall kickstart with a release of the token’s full supply to the public. The marketplace shall also go live with items ready for purchase.
