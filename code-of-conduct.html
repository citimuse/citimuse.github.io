Community Guidelines Updated Feb 27, 2022
I will only sell items that I own

Citimuse enables you to reach millions of customers. We strive to ensure a fair and trustworthy buyer and seller experience. At Citimuse, we expect you to adhere to the code of conduct principles outlined below.

  • Don’t post items that you don't own.
  • Don’t post items that you cannot ship.
  • Don’t post copyrighted or trademarked content.
Preventing Money Laundering

It is critical that we prevent our systems from being used for illegal activities such as money laundering and illicit transactions.

Note: The following situations may be indications of potential money laundering
  • Attempts to make large payments in crypto.
  • Payments by someone who is not a party to the transaction.
  • Requests to pay more than provided for in the contract.
  • Transactions forming an unusual pattern such as bulk purchases of products or many repetitive crypto payments
  • Payments that are substantially higher than the perceived value of an item
Safeguarding our marketplace

We are committed to operating a community marketplace and we should take all reasonable measures to discourage and remove illegal content. If you come across suspicious activities, including listings and transactions that involve illegal items, you should contact DAO team members in discord or send an email to admin@citimuse.com.
