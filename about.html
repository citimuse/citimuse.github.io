The First Decentralized Marketplace

Citimuse is a decentralized marketplace that will provide creators with support to launch their products and allow people to trade conveniently. At the core of the marketplace is decentralization, which is emphasized through the DAO structure that allows holders to contribute to the marketplace decision-making process.

Stay tuned for more updates on the project launch

Vision

At Citimuse, our vision is to create a decentralized marketplace. The marketplace will offer only high-quality listings providing people with the best of items. The marketplace will enable extremely fast and secure disputes to ensure a seamless operation and prevent fraud.

Our vision is to support the work of creators, developers, and participants in our marketplace. We are also committed to allowing people to join the DAO and contribute to the decision-making process. DAO members will also earn commissions on listings.

Core Values

At Citimuse, we operate with and believe in certain principles, which serve as our operational guide. Committed to seeing the world evolve, we have itemized the values which serve as the bedrock of our decentralized marketplace.

  • Commitment to decentralization
  • Fairness to all marketplace participants
  • Future-oriented

We believe that a combination of these principles creates a value system that holds true in the face of accelerating the advent of the world.
