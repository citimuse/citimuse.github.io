The First Decentralized Marketplace

At Citimuse, we're excited about a brand new type of digital good called a non-fungible token, or NFT. NFTs have exciting new properties: theyâ€™re unique, provably scarce, tradeable, and usable across multiple applications. Just like physical goods, you can do whatever you want with them! You could throw them in the trash, gift them to a friend across the world, or go sell them on an open marketplace. But unlike physical goods, they're armed with all the programmability of digital goods.

A core part of our vision is that open protocols like Ethereum and interoperable standards like ERC-721 and ERC-1155 will enable vibrant new economies. We're building tools that allow consumers to trade their items freely, creators to launch new digital works, and developers to build rich, integrated marketplaces for their digital items.

Project Roadmap

THE BEGINNING
This phase marks the beginning of the Project GAIA journey. After establishing the community, we will go live with the Cyborgs minting. Then, 24-48 hours later, we will reveal the Cyborgs to the world and rebuild the Earth on our own rules.
THE UPRISING
In the second phase, we will reveal the Structures. This new, innovative, and gamified NFT experience will keep its' users engaged while maintaining the stunning art quality. This NFT will also be usable as Units in the upcoming Play-to-Earn game based on Project GAIA.
THE REVIVAL
In the third phase, we will introduce a new utility currency that you can use to interact with the Structures NFT collection and future utilities. Upgrade, evolve, or merge your Structures for unpredictable outcomes. Also, use your powerful Structures in the upcoming game.
THE OPULENCE
The last fourth phase indicates the arrival of the Play-to-Earn game based on the Project GAIA lore and art. Use your Structures to beat levels and earn GAIA Tokens or beat your opponents and win what's theirs.
THE FUTURE
Nobody knows what the future holds, but if you help us reach that point, you will undoubtedly have a real impact on the shape of what's coming next. Brace yourselves. The future is coming!
